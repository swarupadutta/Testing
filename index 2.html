Sericulture : mulberry silk

what is mulberry silk? 

the silk producing moth whose lifecycle is solely associated with the mulberry plant
is known as mulberry silk moth.
virtually the laying of eggs by gravid female moth,
hatching of the eggs to larvae,
and the growth of the larvae to form cocoon depend on the mulberry plant.
quality silk produced from the cocoon is known as  mulberry silk

Scientific name of the mulberry silk worms

  1. Bombyx mori
  2. Bombyx fortunatus
  3. Bombyx textor

Systematic position of the mulberry silk worms:

  • phylum : arthropoda
  • Sub phylum : Mandibulata
  • class : Insecta
  • Sub class : Pterygota
  • order : Lepidoptera
